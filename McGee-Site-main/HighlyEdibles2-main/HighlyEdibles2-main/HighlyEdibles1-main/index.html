Introducing The Door of No Return

The Door of No Return

The Door of No Return is our play to earn web based multiplayer NFT game based where holders of the highlyedibles NFT collection will be able to enjoy a fun online experience while earning $MilliTickets! Players will be able to make teams up to 4 and venture through the dangers of the dungeons where they can upgrade their gear, find powerups, get access to extravagant marketplaces and fight bossess to earn $MilliTickets and dive deeper into the dungeons.

  • Users need to be holding at least one of our NFTs
  • Better Geared up NFTs sell for more in the maretplace
  • Upgrade gear in the lobby
  • Unlock keys to deeper dungeons
  • Keep your gear even if you lose!
